Client Services

Flexible Staffing Solutions

Prime Consulting Services can provide the IT talent you need for specific projects, staff augmentation or maintenance on an existing system. Our professional staff is available whether your project requires cutting edge development or maintenance on a legacy system. Whatever your IT project entails, our contractors will function in the role that is the most beneficial to your organization.

Contract to Hire

Also known as the try/buy, this option allows the client to work with the IT person over a specified period of time to make sure that it is a good match for both the organization and the IT professional. All Prime Consultants submitted for this role will be pre-screened to ensure that employment at the client site is the ultimate goal.

Direct Placement

Prime will work with all necessary parties within your organization in order to submit pre-screened talent for your requirements. Our large network, acquired through our years of experience, allows us to recruit those individuals that may not be posting to the job boards.

Consulting Services

Prime can provide expert level personnel when a new technology or update is introduced to your organization.

Clinical Staff

Prime can provide the RN with the experience you need whether it is for a per diem shift or for a long-term assignment. All of our RN's have many years of experience and can "hit the ground running".

Advanced Practice RN

Prime specializes in recruiting Nurse Practitioners for permanent positions and contract opportunities. We recruit for all of you clinical needs from hospitals to clinics in retail locations.
